From Homemaker to Earner — Real Stories from TheBirbal Community

Three incredible women who stepped out of their comfort zones, took a chance on themselves, and transformed their lives through work from home opportunities. Their journeys will inspire you.

TheBirbal Team
Published February 18, 2025

Every woman in our community has a story. A moment when they decided that their potential was worth more than the limitations placed around them. Here are three of those stories — told in their own words.

Story 1 — Kavita, 34, Patna

Kavita had been a homemaker for 6 years after the birth of her second child. With a bachelor's degree in commerce and strong organizational skills, she always felt she had more to offer. But in Patna, opportunities for flexible work were almost nonexistent.

Kavita's Words

"I thought my career was over the day I left my job. Six years at home, and I had almost convinced myself that I had forgotten how to work. When a friend told me about TheBirbal, I was very skeptical. But I applied anyway. Within two weeks, I was doing customer support for a travel company. Within three months, I was team lead. Today I earn more than I ever did in my old job — and I'm home for my children every day."

Today, Kavita manages a team of 12 women and has been felicitated twice by TheBirbal for her outstanding performance. She recently purchased her first laptop with her own earnings — something she describes as the proudest moment of her life.

Story 2 — Meena, 28, Jaipur

Meena's family was conservative. After marriage, she was expected to stop working. She had a degree in English literature and a genuine talent for communication, but the pressure to stay home was immense. Remote work gave her an escape route nobody could argue with.

Meena's Words

"My husband's family didn't want me to work outside. But working from home? That they accepted. I started with TheBirbal doing telecalling for two hours a day. Now I do eight hours and earn ₹24,000 a month. I have my own savings account. I have confidence I never had before. My in-laws are proud of me now. Everything changed."

Meena has since enrolled in an online digital marketing course funded by her own earnings. She plans to eventually lead her own digital business. TheBirbal, she says, was the first door that opened.

Story 3 — Anita, 41, Mumbai Suburbs

Anita's story is different. She had a corporate career — a good one — before a health issue forced her to resign and rest at home for nearly two years. When she recovered, she was 40 and felt the job market had moved on without her. Returning to a full-time office job felt impossible.

Anita's Words

"I had 15 years of sales experience, but after two years out of the market, nobody would hire me. TheBirbal didn't ask me about the gap. They asked me what I could do. I joined as a senior sales specialist and within weeks I was the top performer in my cohort. They saw my potential, not my pause."

Today, Anita trains new joiners at TheBirbal. She has fully reclaimed her career — and in many ways, surpassed where she was before her health break.

What These Stories Tell Us

Kavita, Meena and Anita are not exceptions. They represent thousands of women across India who are talented, capable and motivated — but have been held back by circumstance, geography, family expectations or life events that had nothing to do with their ability.

Remote work, when done right and with the right support, is one of the most powerful tools for women's economic empowerment that has ever existed. At TheBirbal, we're proud to be part of that story.

A career break doesn't define you. What you do next does.
